Lasagna Recipe

Description

This is a fast-to-cook, and inexpensive, lasagna.

Per serving, it contains 643 calories; 41.3g of protein; 53.4g of carbohydrates; 29.3g of fat; 108.3mg of cholesterol, and 707.3mg of sodium.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 (16 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • ½ pound shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ pound shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 pint ricotta cheese
